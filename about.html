About Parkit

Parkit is a smart parking management platform built for the cities of Uttar Pradesh. We believe that finding and booking a parking spot should be as easy as ordering food online — instant, reliable, and stress-free.

🎯 Our Mission

Urban congestion is one of the biggest challenges facing Indian cities today. A significant portion of city traffic is caused by drivers circling blocks looking for parking. Parkit aims to eliminate that wasted time.

We connect drivers to available parking slots in real-time, enabling advance bookings, instant payments, and hassle-free cancellations — all from a single app.

Starting with the major cities of Uttar Pradesh — Lucknow, Varanasi, Agra, Noida, and Prayagraj — we plan to expand across India.
